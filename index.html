Research Experiences

  • Aug.2013 - Aug 2014: Research intern @ Speech Group, Microsoft Research Asia with Dr. Soong and Dr. Yao
    During the internship, my research focused on how to improve performance of the Neural Network based voice conversion, including 1) DBN or layer-wise BP pre-training for Neural Network 2) Change training criterion from Minimum Frame Error to Minimum Sequence Error 3) Wavelet decomposed pitch conversion in Neural Network frame work The details can be seen in [2].
  • Jan.2012 - Jun.2012: Research intern @ Speech Group, Microsoft Research Asia with Dr. Soong and Yi-Jian Wu on improving the model clustering result for HMM-based TTS
    During the internship, I was trying to generalize cross validation to decision tree clustering with Minimum Generation Error training criterion to improve the robustness of HMM-based TTS and alleviate the over-fitting problem associated with the conventional approach without using CV. The details can be seen in [1].
